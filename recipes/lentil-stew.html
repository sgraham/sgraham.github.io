Lentil Stew (Instant Pot)

Ingredients

  • 1 onion diced
  • 7 cloves garlic diced
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 4 C vegetables (potato, pepper, carrot, celery, …)
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 1 Tbsp cumin
  • 2 tsp coriander
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp herbes de Provence
  • 2 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 medium tomatoes diced (or 14.5 oz can)
  • 4 C bouillon
  • 2 C water
  • 1.25 C green lentils

    Directions

  • Saute onion and garlic in oil in Instant Pot for 2-3min
  • Add veggies, spices, salt, saute for 5 min more
  • Add tomato paste, tomatoes, bouillon, water, lentils
  • Pressure cook for 25 min
