Pea Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 C dried green split peas
  • 6 C water
  • 1 large sweet potato (~2 C)
  • 1 large onion (~1 C)
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 3 C spinach leaves
  • 1 C half-and-half or coconut cream/milk
  • pepper for serving

    • Directions

  • Mix split peas, water, sweet potato, onion, garlic, and salt in slow cooker
  • Cook 10-11 hours on low
  • Stir in spinach and milk, and low for 30 min
  • Blend if desired and serve with pepper
