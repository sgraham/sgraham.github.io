Waffles

Ingredients

  • 3 C flour
  • 3 Tbsp sugar
  • 3 Tbsp baking powder
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 3 C almond milk
  • 3 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 3 tsp vanilla

    • Directions

  • Mix dry in one bowl
  • Whisk wet in another bowl
  • Combine and whisk until smooth
  • Let rest 5 minutes
  • Cook in oiled fry pan or waffle iron
