Waffles

Ingredients

  • 1 C flour
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 Tbsp baking powder
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 1 C almond milk
  • 1 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp vanilla

    • Directions

  • Mix dry in one bowl
  • Whisk wet in another bowl
  • Combine and whisk until smooth
  • Let rest 5 minutes
  • Cook in oiled fry pan or waffle iron
