Creamy Navy Bean Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 C dried white navy beans
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 2 large carrots
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 3 C unsweetened soy milk
  • 3 C vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp rosemary
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper

    • Directions

  • Dice onion, celery, carrots, sauté in oil for 5min
  • Add garlic for another min
  • Add rinced beans, spices, soy milk, and broth
  • High pressure cook for 35min, immersion blend a bit
