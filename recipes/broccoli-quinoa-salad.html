Broccoli and Quinoa Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 C cooked quinoa
  • 2 C chopped broccoli florets
  • 1/4 C roasted sunflower seeds
  • 1/4 C mayonnaise
  • 1/4 C plain yogurt
  • 1 tsp grated lemon zest
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp honey
  • pinch salt
  • pinch pepper

    Directions

    Mix and toss
