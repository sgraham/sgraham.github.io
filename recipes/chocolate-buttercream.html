Chocolate Buttercream

Ingredients

  • 1 C unsalted butter softened to room temperature
  • 3 1/2 C powdered sugar
  • 2/3 C cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/8 - 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3-4 Tbsp heavy cream or milk plus more as needed

    Directions

  • Beat butter on low-speed until creamy and smooth. Scrape the sides and bottom of the bowl.
  • Add 2 C of powdered sugar, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, 1/8 tsp salt and 3 Tbsp of heavy cream and mix on low speed until well combined.
  • Gradually with mixer on low-speed add remaining powdered sugar. The icing may appear pretty dry, this is OK.
