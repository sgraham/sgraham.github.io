Icing

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp butter, softened
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 Tbsp milk
  • 2 C icing sugar
  • 4-5 Tbsp potato starch

    Directions

  • Beat butter in medium bowl until creamy (30-45s)
  • Add vanilla extract and milk and continue beating
  • Slowly add icing sugar 1/2 C at a time
  • Beat for another 2-3 mins
  • Add potato starch 2 Tbsp at a time and combine
