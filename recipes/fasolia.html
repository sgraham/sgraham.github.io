Fasolia

Ingredients

  • 24 oz tomato sauce
  • 28 oz diced tomatoes
  • 5-7 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 2 onions
  • 2 C dried lima beans
  • salt, pepper, cinnamon

    Directions

  • Rinse and pressure cook lima beans in lots of water, 25 min on high
  • Alternatively, soak beans in lots of water with baking soda overnight and drain/rinse before mixing in
  • Saute onion in olive oil in large pot until starting to caramelize
  • Add garlic, saute for a minute longer
  • Add salt, pepper, cinnamon to taste, more cinnamon than you would expect
  • Add tomato sauce and tomatoes and simmer for 10-15 min, adjust for taste
  • Add beans and continue to simmer
  • Serve with vermicelli rice and fresh arabic pita
