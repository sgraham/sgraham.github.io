Miso Gravy

Ingredients

  • 4 Tbsp red miso paste, or 6 to 8 Tbsp yellow or white miso
  • 2 C veggie stock
  • 2 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp chili paste
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 Tbsp grated ginger
  • Flour or corn starch and water for thickening
