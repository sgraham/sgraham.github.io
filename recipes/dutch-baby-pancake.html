Dutch Baby Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 1 1/3 C milk
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 C flour
  • 1 Tbsp butter

    Directions

  • Preheat oven to 425
  • Whisk together eggs, milk, sugar, vanilla
  • Whisk in flour
  • Heat iron pan with butter
  • Pour batter in and cook for 10 mins
