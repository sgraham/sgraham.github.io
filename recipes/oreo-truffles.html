Oreo Truffles

Ingredients

  • 36 Oreos
  • 1 pkg (250g) cream cheese
  • 2 pkgs baker's chocolate or semi-sweet chips

    Directions

  • Don't double recipe or food processor will choke
  • Blend cookies first
  • Then blend in cream cheese
  • Form balls onto waxed paper surface, and then refrigerate
  • Melt chocolate, and dip balls
  • Store in freezer or fridge
