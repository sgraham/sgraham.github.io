Slowcooker Pinto Beans

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs dried pinto beans (3C)
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 small yellow onion diced
  • 1 large pepper diced
  • 1.5 tsp kosher salt—divided
  • 5 cloves minced garlic
  • 1.5 tsp herbes de Provence
  • 1.5 tsp ground cumin
  • 1.5 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/8 to 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 5 C water
  • 6 C broth

    Directions

  • Rinse beans and put in slow cooker
  • Heat oil, add onion, pepper, and .5 tsp of salt
  • Saute for 2 min and add garlic, saute for 30s, add to slowcooker
  • Add spices and rest of salt, broth and water
  • Cook on HIGH 12 hours
