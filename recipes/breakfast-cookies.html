Breakfast Cookies (doubled)

Ingredients

  • 2 C quick oats
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 C peanut butter
  • 2/3 C apple sauce
  • 2 large banana, mashed (about 1 C)
  • 1 C dried cranberries
  • 1 C pumpkin seeds
  • 1 C raisins
  • 1 C nuts

    Directions

  • Use hand mixer to mix all ingredients
  • Cook 16-18 min at 325 F
